The UCSF History website is a collaboration of the UCSF Library and Center for Knowledge Management and the UCSF Department of Anthropology, History, and Social Medicine.

Funding for the project was provided by the Office of the Executive Vice Chancellor and Provost, A. Eugene Washington.

The cooperation and support of the UCSF Schools of Dentistry, Medicine, Nursing, and Pharmacy, the Graduate Division, and the UCSF Medical Center was vital to the success of the project.
